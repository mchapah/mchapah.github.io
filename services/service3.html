Merchant Portal
This is an web-based tool that provides a full view of a transaction lifecycle to the merchants and ensuresthat merchants are in full control of transactions they process/acquire. Through the portal, merchants can;
  • View all approved card transactions and e-commerce transactions
  • Generate statistics and view graphs of financial statements
  • Administer users
  • Initiate Charge back and other back office processing
  • Submit service requests
  • View turnover history
