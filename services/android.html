Android Applications
This is a one-stop mobile payments and digital commerce service for businesses, mobile network operators, financial institutions and consumers that has;
  • Mobile banking
  • Agency Application
  • Card Issuing platform – Specifically for distribution of pre-paid cards and/or SIM cards through the bank agency network
  • Consumer Portal – linked to card and consumer account
  • 3rd party payments
  • Merchant Application
  • Intergration to billers
  • E-vouchers
  • Remote Account opening
