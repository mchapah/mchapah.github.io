  • Electronic Document Management Systems

    • Our EDMS software provides a solution that manages the creation, storage and control of documents electronically. The primary function of our EDMS is to manage electronic information within an organization workflow. The EDMS includes document management, workflow, text retrieval, and imaging. It is capable of providing secure access, maintaining the context, and executing disposition instructions for all records in the system.

  • Fleet/Fuel Card Management Systems

    • Our Fleet/Fuel card management system has hardware and software complex for management and accounting of fuel sales at a single petrol station or a petrol station network using RFID fuel cards with fuel volume, money and discount wallets or their combinations. The system allows to replace usage of cash and coupons to more reliable and convenient solution using fuel and money cards. The fleet card system can be used by customers for petrol refilling and allows the customer to earn loyalties or discounts The system allows to issue fuel and discount cards for customers and also the cards management and accounting of all sales using fuel cards, to manage customer accounts, and to set flexible discount system.

  • Imaging and Recognition Solutions

    • Our Mobile Capture Imaging and Recognition system powers the document capture module of mobile applications to ensure document capture and data extraction work correctly the first time, every time, providing your customers with the frictionless mobile capture experience they expect. The system controls lighting conditions, camera focus, provides guidance instructions, and auto-captures the document. After the document is captured, image-perfection technology cleans up, de-skews, and reduces the image size. This optimizes the image for both data extraction and network transmission, resulting in a near real-time experience.
